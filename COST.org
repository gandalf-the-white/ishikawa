#+title: Cost

* Cost function
** Likelihood (/Vraisemblance/)
  #+begin_src latex
\begin{align*}
  L &=\prod_{i=1}^mP(Y=y_i)\\
    &=\prod_{i=1}^ma_i^{y_i}\times(1-a_i)^{1-y_i}
\end{align*}
  #+end_src
** Loss function (Log Loss)
#+begin_src latex
\begin{equation*}
  \mathcal{L}=-\frac{1}{m}\sum_{i=1}^m(y_ilog(a_i)+(1-y)log(1-a_i))
\end{equation*}
#+end_src
** Log Likelihood
  #+begin_src latex
\begin{align*}
    LL &=log(\prod_{i=1}^m(a_i^{y_i}\times(1-a_i)^{1-y_i})\\
       &=\sum_{i=1}^m(log(a_i^{y_i})+log(1-a_i)^{1-y_i})\\
       &=\sum_{i=1}^m(y_ilog(a_i)+(1-y_i)log(1-a_i))
\end{align*}
  #+end_src
** Result
  #+begin_src latex
\begin{equation*}
\mathcal{L}=-\frac{1}{m}\times(LL)
\end{equation*}
  #+end_src
